Adjustment Weights Pros Only
⚠ These weights feed every comp adjustment directly into the ARV. Only change them with market-derived support (paired sales, local $/sqft studies) — a wrong weight silently skews every valuation. The subject is valued in after-repair (renovated) condition; comps in weaker condition adjust upward to match. Sequencing follows appraiser practice: % adjustments apply to each comp's time-adjusted price; renovated comps take no age adjustment (renovation resets effective age); the sqft line is net of bedroom/bath footprints so rooms aren't paid for twice.
